Technology

How It Works

A complete air conditioning system — compressor, condenser, and evaporator — engineered into a single compact unit. No outdoor equipment. No refrigerant lines to run.

Cooled Supply Air
Warm Return Air
Hot Exhaust (to outside)
Fresh Air Intake

Truly Invisible

Mounts entirely inside the ceiling, wall, or floor. Nothing visible from outside the building. Only discreet supply grilles are seen indoors.

Quick Retrofit

No refrigerant lines to run between units. No outdoor pad, bracket, or structural support needed. Dramatically faster installation than split systems.

Flexible Ducting

Up to 4 supply air outputs with 100mm flexible duct connections. Return air from either side. Configure for any room layout.

Fresh Air Options

Three ventilation modes: 100% recirculated, 50/50 mixed, or 100% fresh outdoor air. Meets health and safety ventilation requirements.

Heat Recovery

Proprietary condensate heat recovery system recycles moisture energy to boost cooling efficiency and reduce electricity consumption.

Full Metal Build

Aluminum alloy and galvanized steel construction. Corrosion resistant, powder coated. Built for decades of reliable operation in any environment.

About UKT

Engineering Climate Solutions Since 1998

United Klima Technologies is a Michigan-based HVAC manufacturer specializing in systems that require no outdoor condenser unit. Our engineering team brings together expertise from the United States, Europe, and Israel to solve a problem that traditional HVAC manufacturers have ignored: how to provide real air conditioning when you simply cannot install an outdoor unit.

Every UKT system is designed and assembled in Michigan. Our products serve residential, commercial, government, and mobile applications across North America and internationally.
