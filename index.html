Technology

Everything inside.
Nothing outside.

A complete air conditioning system — compressor, condenser, and evaporator — in one compact unit that fits inside your ceiling. The only things that pass through the wall are two air ducts behind a discreet louver.

Cooled supply air
Warm return air
Hot exhaust (to outside)
Fresh outdoor air

Truly invisible

Mounts entirely inside the ceiling. Only discreet supply grilles are visible indoors. Nothing on the exterior.

Quick retrofit

No refrigerant lines between units. No outdoor pad or structural support. Dramatically faster than split systems.

Flexible ducting

Up to 4 supply outputs with 100 mm flex duct connections. Return air from either side. Any room layout.

Fresh air modes

Three ventilation modes — recirculated, 50/50 mixed, or 100% fresh outdoor air. Meets ventilation codes.

Heat recovery

Proprietary condensate heat recovery recycles moisture energy to boost efficiency and lower electricity use.

Full metal build

Aluminum alloy and galvanized steel. Corrosion-resistant, powder-coated. Built for decades of operation.

About

Engineering climate solutions since 1998.

United Klima Technologies is a Michigan-based HVAC manufacturer specializing in systems that require no outdoor condenser unit. Our engineering team brings together expertise from the United States, Europe, and Israel to solve a problem traditional HVAC manufacturers have ignored: how to provide real air conditioning when you simply cannot install an outdoor unit.

Every UKT system is designed and assembled in Michigan. Our products serve residential, commercial, government, and mobile applications across North America and internationally.
